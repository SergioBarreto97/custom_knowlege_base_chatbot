The Following Are Definitions For WallStreetOdds Filters/Metrics That Fall Under The Analyst Ratings Downgrades Category.
# Of Downgrades In The Last Day: The number of analyst downgrades that a stock received in the last 24 hours.
# Of Downgrades In The Last Week: The number of analyst downgrades that a stock received in the last week.
# Of Downgrades In The Last Month: The number of analyst downgrades that a stock received in the last month.
# Of Downgrades In The Last Year: The number of analyst downgrades that a stock received in the last year.
