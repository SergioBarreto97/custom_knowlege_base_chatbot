The Following Are Definitions For WallStreetOdds Filters/Metrics That Fall Under The Price Change Since Highs Category.
% Below 1 Week High: The percentage (%) difference between the current price of an asset and the highest price it traded at in the previous 5 trading days.
% Below 1 Month High: The percentage (%) difference between the current price of an asset and the highest price it traded at in the previous 23 trading days.
% Below 1 Year High: The percentage (%) difference between the current price of an asset and the highest price it traded at in the previous 252 trading days.
% Below 10 Year High: The percentage (%) difference between the current price of an asset and the highest price it traded at in the previous 2,520 trading days.
